Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 February 2026

Introduction

Sanctuary Study is a personal project created by Treasure Okoye. This Privacy Policy explains how personal data is collected, used, and protected when you use Sanctuary Study.

What Data We Collect

We may collect the following data:

  • First name
  • Last name
  • Email address
  • Password (managed through Firebase Authentication)
  • Optional date of birth
  • Study usage data such as sessions, achievements, and basic analytics events

How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Sign you in securely (including Google sign-in if you choose it)
  • Save your study progress, streaks, and achievements
  • Understand which features are used so the app can be improved

Legal Basis (UK GDPR)

Under UK GDPR, our main legal bases are:

  • Performance of a contract: to provide account access and core app features you request.
  • Legitimate interests: to maintain, secure, and improve the app for personal project use.

How and Where Data Is Stored

Sanctuary Study uses Firebase services provided by Google, including Firebase Authentication and potentially Firestore and/or Analytics. Data is stored in these services as needed to run the app.

For these services, Google/Firebase acts as a processor or service provider for the technical delivery of the app.

Who We Share Data With

We do not sell your personal data. Data is only shared with Firebase/Google as necessary to operate Sanctuary Study.

Data Retention

Your data is kept while your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and associated personal data at any time.

Your Rights Under UK GDPR

You may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Request portability of your data
  • Request restriction of processing
  • Object to certain processing
  • Complain to the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO)

Cookies and Local Storage

Sanctuary Study may use cookies and/or browser local storage for authentication state, app settings, and optional analytics-related behavior. This is used to keep the app functional and improve user experience.
